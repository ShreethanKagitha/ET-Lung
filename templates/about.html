Clinical Ethics & Medical Disclaimer

This platform functions strictly as a computational risk stratification utility. It synthesizes statistical correlations based on user-provided vectors against trained datasets. It must never supersede, augment, or replace standardized medical procedures, radiological scans, or professional oncological diagnostics.

Outputs should be viewed as probabilistic indicators. Authorized medical professionals should review all flagged risk factors in accordance with established medical protocols.
