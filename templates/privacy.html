Privacy Policy

Zero-trust data security protocols for clinical confidentiality.

1. Data Collection & Usage

When utilizing the Pulmonary Intelligence Platform, predictive form data (such as age, pack-years, and medical history) is collected solely for the transient execution of real-time machine learning inference. As per HIPAA compliance simulations, this data is never permanently retained on our servers post-prediction.

2. Voice Recognition Security

Audio inputs captured via the Voice Assistant leverage Web Speech Recognition APIs. Audio transcripts are piped securely to local GenAI (Gemini) layers via an encrypted bridge strictly to parse structural JSON required for ML inputs. Audio files themselves are never recorded or stored.

3. User Accounts

If you opt to register an account to view your dashboard, your passwords are cryptographically hashed using bcrypt. At no time are plaintext passwords accessible to administrators or third parties. Prediction history histories tied to accounts can be deleted upon user request.

4. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or lease clinical diagnostic vectors to any third-party marketing entities. Our APIs may transmit anonymized context strictly for RAG-grounded LLM inference without attached personally identifiable information (PII).

End-to-End Encryption

This platform is built on modern Web3-inspired privacy architectures. Your health data remains fundamentally yours.
